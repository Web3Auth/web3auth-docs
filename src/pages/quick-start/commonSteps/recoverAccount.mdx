---
title: Recover MFA Enabled Account
---

For the accounts that have enabled MFA, you need to recover the account by inputting their Backup
Factor Key while logging in.

:::tip

In this example, we're directly entering the backup factor key of the user, you can also do it via
decrypting your backup factor encrypted by other common methods like SMS OTP or Email Passwordless.

:::

:::note SDK Reference

- [Installing Web3Auth](/sdk/mpc-core-kit/mpc-core-kit-js/usage#enable-mfa).

:::
