title: Making Blockchain Calls
---

Web3Auth is chain agnostic. This means that you can use it with any blockchain with the provider exposed by Web3Auth. For EVM, Solana and XRPL
Blockchain Web3Auth exposes special providers for native integrations.

:::tip Reference

Have a look at our [Connect Blockchain](/connect-blockchain/) section of the documentation and choose your blockchain to get started.

- [Solana](/connect-blockchain/solana)
- [XRPL](/connect-blockchain/xrpl)
- [Other Blockchains](/connect-blockchain/)

:::
