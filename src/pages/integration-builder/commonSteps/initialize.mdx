---
title: Initializing
---

Finally, it is time to initialise the SDK within your application. Here is where you choose your logins, whether to login with popups or redirects,
whitelabel config and more. You can use the default set of logins & wallets (adapters), by not passing any argument into the function or choose the
specifics for yourself.

> More about initialising Web3Auth and its parameters [here](/sdk/web/modal/initialize).
