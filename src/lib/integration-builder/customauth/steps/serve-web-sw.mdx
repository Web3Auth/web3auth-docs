---
title: Serve service worker (sw.js)
---

Serve
[sw.js](https://github.com/torusresearch/customauth/blob/master/serviceworker/sw.js)
from `baseUrl` where `baseUrl` is the one that you provided to `DirectWebSdk` in
the previous step.

If you're already using a service worker, please ensure to port over the fetch
override.
