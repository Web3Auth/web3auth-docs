## Adding the Dynamics of Web3 to Your DApp

Integrating with Torus gives you a provider that you can wrap the Web3 around.
This gives you a web3 instance to play with, read more on
[Web3's Documentation](https://web3js.readthedocs.io/en/1.0/). You can implement
web3 functionalities just like you would with Metamask.
