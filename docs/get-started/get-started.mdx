**This guide will help you get started with Web3Auth and navigate around our documentation to help you integrate quickly!**

## Basic Installation

---

If you are new to Web3Auth, there are only a few steps you have to complete to start using it.

1. Sign up and create an account on the [Web3Auth Dashboard](https://dashboard.web3auth.io/). This is where you will be setting up and managing your
   projects and managing them end to end. You will also be able to see your project's statistics and see the latest activity.

2. Based on your app's stack and usecase, choose one of our [Quick Starts](./quickstart#select-your-language-framework), and hook it up to your app.

3. Configure the app for your specific blockchain Include final touches by [connecting with your blockchain](./quickstart#connect-with-blockchains).

4. Add final touches by
   [customising your Login UI and/or including your own Authentication Service](./quickstart#use-a-custom-authentication-and-login-ui-for-your-application)
   with your Web3Auth instance.

:::

## Exploring a more Customised Integration

---

More customising your integration further, we might recommend you the following

1. Explore the capabilities of [our dashboard](https://dashboard.web3auth.io/) and how you can use it to manage your project
2. Learn about our [different SDKs](/developing-with-web3auth/understand-sdk) available and what are the features you can utilise in each of them
3. Understand the key fundamentals development with Web3Auth

- Customising your application with [Adapters](/developing-with-web3auth/adapters)
- Use [verifiers](/developing-with-web3auth/customauth/verifiers) for using your [own authentication service](/developing-with-web3auth/customauth/)
- Know about the different available providers and how you can [connect any blockchain](/developing-with-web3auth/connect-blockchain) with Web3Auth

4. See the process of [Whitelabeling](/whitelabeling) your Web3Auth instance to suit the UI of your application
5. Checkout our thorough [API Reference](/api-reference/) which covers all the methods and properties of Web3Auth SDKs

- [Learn more about **How Web3Auth works?**](/overview/how-web3auth-works) in the backend of your application
- [Experience a Production Application that is using us](/examples#production-examples)

:::
